About Me

My name is Emma Gail and I am currently a PhD student at Monash University in Melbourne, Australia. My current research interests involve creating workflows to better understand protein interactions, especially in regard to one of the focuses of my current lab: PRC2 and its accessory subunits. This is the home to my publicly available code/documentation and related updates.
